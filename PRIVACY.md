## English

### 1. Scope

This policy applies to the Nida App (iOS, Android, and similar builds) and to **public** configuration and media files served from the repository **ismile94/nidaunnur-content** (for example via **jsDelivr** and **raw.githubusercontent.com**). It does **not** apply to third-party websites you open through in-app links (those sites have their own policies).

### 2. Accounts

The App is designed to work **without creating a user account**. We do not operate a traditional “sign up / log in” profile inside the App for general use.

### 3. Categories of data and purposes

Depending on your choices and device permissions, the App may process:

- **Location (approximate or precise):** To compute **prayer times** for your place, show **Qibla** direction, load **map** views, suggest **nearby places of worship**, and related time-zone or routing helpers. Location-related requests may be sent to **prayer-calculation APIs** (including **Aladhan** calendar endpoints with coordinates), **Google Maps Platform** services (Maps, Places, Directions, Time Zone API as used by the App), or other network services the App calls for these features. If you deny location access, you may still be able to use **manual city / coordinate** entry where the App supports it.

- **Locally stored preferences:** The App may store settings (for example language, prayer preferences, notification toggles, cached prayer times) on your device using mechanisms such as **local storage / SharedPreferences**. This data stays on the device unless a feature explicitly sends it to a server.

- **Notifications:** Where you enable them, the App may schedule **local notifications** on your device. Content needed for those notifications is processed on-device or prepared locally; we do not operate a generic “push notification account” for advertising in the App’s core design described here.

- **Usage analytics (aggregated):** The App may use **Firebase Analytics** (Google) to collect **aggregated** usage and diagnostic signals to understand feature usage and stability. This is intended to avoid collecting unnecessary personally identifying details; however, analytics providers may receive technical identifiers typical of mobile analytics (such as device/app instance identifiers) according to their own infrastructure.

- **Fonts and media:** The App may download **fonts** (for example via **Google Fonts** packaging used in the project) or **audio / images** from configured hosts. Remote **seasonal content** and translations may be loaded from **GitHub / CDN** endpoints pointing at **nidaunnur-content** or similar public URLs.

- **Diagnostics you choose to send:** If you use a “report problem” or support flow, any information you include in that message (including contact details you type) is handled for **support** purposes.

### 4. Legal bases (where GDPR-style concepts apply)

We rely on **performance of the service you request** (prayer times, maps, reminders), **your consent** (permissions such as location or notifications), and **legitimate interests** (security, fraud prevention, product improvement through aggregated analytics), as applicable under local law.

### 5. Sharing and processors

We do not sell your personal information. The App may transmit data to **service providers / infrastructure** needed to operate features, including but not limited to:

- **Google** (Firebase Analytics, Maps Platform, fonts or other Google-hosted assets as configured),
- **Aladhan** (or successor endpoints) for prayer-time calculations,
- **GitHub / jsDelivr** (public content delivery),
- **Apple** / **Google** as platform vendors for distribution, crash/diagnostic tooling they provide on-device, and their applicable store policies.

Those providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

### 6. Retention

- **On-device** preferences and caches persist until you clear app data / uninstall or the App overwrites them.
- **Server-side** retention (if any) for analytics or APIs depends on each provider’s policies.

### 7. Your choices

You can **withdraw permissions** in your device settings (location, notifications, etc.), use **manual location** where supported, **limit ad tracking / analytics** options the platform offers, or **uninstall** the App.

### 8. Children

The App is not directed at children to collect data from them beyond what is reasonably needed to provide general utility features. Parents or guardians should supervise device permissions.

### 9. International transfers

Providers may process data in countries other than your own. We rely on standard contractual mechanisms / provider safeguards where required.

### 10. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change; material changes may also be reflected in store listings or in-app notices where appropriate.

### 11. Contact

Questions or requests (including access/deletion where applicable): **ismailaram94@gmail.com**
